Keeping Model T Windows Clear in Rainy Weather

Many Model Ts do not have windshield wipers to keep their windows clear when it starts to rain. Wipe the windows with Rain-X and the water drops will roll off the windows a little easier.

Contributed by Ernie Wentrcek ~ January 2008
